Exploring the project

Introduction

I made a game looks like a Doom game thanks to the @CoderSpaceChannel's video. I want to make this project a game you can play without necessarily knowing the story behind it. My basic idea was that I wanted to give myself the challenge of making a game without a graphics engine, in Python, and with virtually no knowledge. One of the most complicated challenges was finding a name. Without lying to you, I'm still not sure of the name. Did you succeed? Will I continue with the game? You be the judge... I'd just like to remind you that this game is in pre-alpha and that Doom's textures won't be definitive.

Features

A simple story that explains the scenario

Old-school gameplay with (I hope) some new elements

Easy to learn "How to play"
